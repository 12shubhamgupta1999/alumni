Alumni Tracking System

The mere purpose behind developing any application is to perceive the needs of some set of potential users, something which is reliable and easily understandable by all. The idea behind the Application is to maintain a mechanism for the Colleges and the Directorate to keep a track of the students passing out. The Aim is to develop a Web Based Application for the Colleges and The Directorate to keep a track of the Alumni.
